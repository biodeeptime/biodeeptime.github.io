Contributing databases

BioDeepTime compiles assemblage time series data from a number of databases - all of which are publicly available.

BioTIME

A global database of modern assemblage time series for quantifying and understanding biodiversity change.

Representative(s): Amelia Penny - Maria Dornelas

Foram sediment traps

High temporal resolution records of planktonic foraminifera assemblages from the modern ocean.

Representative(s): Lukas Jonkers

Geobiodiversity Database

Outcrop based list of

Representative(s): Lin Na - Qijian Li

MarBen

Assemblage time series from the marine benthos in shallow geological time.

Representative(s): Adam Tomasovych - Huai-Hsuan (May) Huang - Moriaki Yasuhara

The Neotoma Paleoecology Database

The largest database of palinomorph time series.

Representative(s): Jack Williams - David Fastovich

Neptune Sandbox Berlin

Representative(s): Johan Renaudie

The Paleobiology Database

A community-driven database of published fossil biogeographical observations (occurrences).

Representative(s): Seth Finnegan - Wolfgang Kiessling

The Triton Database

A curated database of planktonic foraminifera from the Cenozoic.

Representative(s): Erin Saupe
